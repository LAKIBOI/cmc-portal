About CMC Portal

The Colombo Municipal Council Complaint Management System is a digital initiative to streamline citizen grievances across roads, water supply, sanitation, electrical, and general services.

🏗️
Infrastructure
Roads, drainage, street lighting and civil works managed end-to-end.
💧
Water & Sanitation
Water supply issues and sanitation complaints resolved within 3 days.
⚡
Electrical
Street lighting and electrical faults across the Colombo district.
📊
Transparent Tracking
Real-time status updates from submission to resolution.

Our Services

🛣️
Roads & Pavements
Pothole repairs, cracked pavements and road maintenance.
💧
Water Supply
Low pressure, discolouration, leaks and pipe bursts.
🗑️
Garbage Collection
Missed pickups, bulk waste and construction debris.
💡
Street Lighting
Non-working lights, flickering and damaged poles.
🌊
Drainage
Blocked drains, flooding and collapsed drainage systems.
🏛️
General Services
Administrative issues, parks and public space complaints.
